Green Hill Swing Festival

  • When? 29th Aug 2024 - 1st Sep 2024
  • Country: Czech Republic
  • Town: Žďár nad Sázavou
  • Website: https://swinguzdar.cz/en/
  • Styles: Jazz, Lindy Hop

Green Hill Swing Festival invites dancers to a chateau in the heart of Czechia. Look forward to international teachers, 4 levels of Lindy Hop classes, 2 parties with live music, special blues stage, 8+ extra workshops, brunch party and a trip to explore the beautiful surroundings.

Teachers:

Barbara Joó & Ján Kubriczký Diana Geisler & Guillaume Roubille Anya Lebedeva & Martin Kurečka
