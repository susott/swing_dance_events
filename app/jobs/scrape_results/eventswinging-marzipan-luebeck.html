Swinging Marzipan Lübeck

  • When? 8th Nov 2024 - 10th Nov 2024
  • Country: Germany
  • Town: Lübeck
  • Website: http://www.swingingmarzipan.de
  • Styles: Lindy Hop

As Lübeck is famous for its Marzipan, we are happy to invite you to swing your marzipan with us. We are happy to invite you to our fourth edition of Swinging Marzipan 2024. Our festival takes place on the 08th -10th November 2024 for a 3-day long swing dances festival with parties and classes in the lovely city of Lübeck. Get your marzipan ready! Registration will start on the 2nd of June
