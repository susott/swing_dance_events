Ladies and Gentlemen, Spirit of St. Louis Rome is proud to present the sixth edition of 'Swingala' , a Lindy Hop and Solo Jazz Festival that will light up Roman nights. Swingala will take place in Rome on September 20th-21th-22th 2024. In these days, our feet won't stand still, thanks to classes, live music and social dance!
