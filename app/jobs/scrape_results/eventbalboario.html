Balboario

  • When? 23rd Aug 2024 - 25th Aug 2024
  • Country: Italy
  • Town: Brescia
  • Website: http://www.swingdancebrescia.com
  • Styles: Balboa

Immersed in the greenery of the Parco delle Terme di Boario (BS) at the gates of the cool Valcamonica, a summer camp dedicated entirely to Balboa will come to life! Neus and Javi with Anna and Gasper will give life to a series of lessons that will be held on Saturday and Sunday afternoons and will offer us a lesson in musicality in the early evening on Saturday. The standard lessons will be set on two levels: Intermediate and advanced and, as in all the workshops we organize, there will be no change of couple during the lessons. There will be DJ sets and Live bands to let us go wild on the dance floor for all three evenings.
