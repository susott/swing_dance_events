Swim Out Costa Brava

  • When? 5th Sep 2024 - 8th Sep 2024
  • Country: Spain
  • Town: Llafranc
  • Website: www.swimoutcostabrava.com
  • Styles: Jazz, Lindy Hop

Spend the last days of summer with us at Costa Brava. Lindy Hop, solo jazz and afrofusion classes with top instructors, neverending parties with Swing Shouters and Shirt Tail Stompers, jam sessions at the pool, a kayak trip, lectures and workshops and the best Mediterranean vibes. Accommodation and all meals included! Registrations open on April 2 at 9 pm CEST.

Teachers:

Laura Glaess, AJ Howard, Pamela Gaizutyte, Tadas Vasiliauskas, Ariadna Beltran, Simon Bressanelli, Viktor Lillard, Tyedric Hill, Alice Mei, Mamy and more TBC!
