Cadiz Swing Festival

  • When? 11th Oct 2024 - 13th Oct 2024
  • Country: Spain
  • Town: Cadiz
  • Website: http://www.cadizswingfestival.com
  • Styles: Lindy Hop

Come dance in Cadiz in Autumn, you can go to the beach during the day, take classes and go to parties! Southern Spain is a unique place, you can get to the city by train from Seville and Madrid!

Teachers:

Joseph Sewell and Charlotte Middlemiss. Organized by Sevilla Swing Dance and SonyLus.
