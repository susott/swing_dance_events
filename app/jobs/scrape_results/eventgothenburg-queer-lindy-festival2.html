Gothenburg Queer Lindy Festival

  • When? 20th Sep 2024 - 22nd Sep 2024
  • Country: Sweden
  • Town: Gothenburg
  • Website: https://www.gbgqueerlindy.com/
  • Styles: Lindy Hop

A lindy hop festival focusing on norm breaking dancing, which's purpose is to let everyone feel included regardless of gender, sexuality and leader/follow preferences. Open for lgbtai+-dancers and allies.
