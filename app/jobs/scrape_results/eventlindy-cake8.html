Lindy Cake goes to chocolate factory This year we are allowing ourselves to be inspired by the wacky tale of Charlie and the Chocolate Factory. Come prepared for sweets, weirdness and plenty of good old-fashioned song and dance! Chocolate bars, a golden ticket and a silver lift: all the ingredients needed for an exciting event on the edge of the Black Forest. Join us together with The Hot Swing Sextet The Hop'Sh Bam Connection and Gordon Webster! Oompa Luumpa and lets go!
