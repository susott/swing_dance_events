All Lithuanian Weekend

  • When? 11th Oct 2024 - 13th Oct 2024
  • Country: Lithuania
  • Town: Vilnius
  • Website: https://alllithuanianweekend.lt
  • Styles: Jazz, Lindy Hop

This is a celebration of the spirit of Jazz Age in the very best way - the way "Lithuanians do it". Lindy hop and solo jazz classes tracks, 4 swing bands, 15+ dance teachers, 20+ hours of social dancing at 3 parties with live band music, competitions and performances! Live bands: Home Jazz Band, The Schwings Band, Collaborative Jazz Septet, Special Delivery Band

Teachers:

Simona Pogosian & Mindaugas Bikauskas, Kamile Pundziute & Smiltys Andrikonis, Elze Visnevskyte & Arnas Jasiunas, Migle Cesiunaite & Vilmantas Balynas, Ruta Raginyte & Rokas Borusevicius, Edita Kriauciunaite & Edas Matulaitis, Egle Treciokaite & Arturas Kisielius
