Are You Syncopated?!

  • When? 11th Oct 2024 - 13th Oct 2024
  • Country: Germany
  • Town: Berlin
  • Website: http://www.areyousyncopated.de
  • Styles: Balboa, Blues, Jazz, Lindy Hop, Shag

'Are You Syncopated?!'' aims for the sweet spot between a dance and a music festival in the space of early jazz and swing, with a strong emphasis on musicality. This year we want to reflect the work of the Syncopated Sisters and we have one big headline in the work! We will also be welcoming guests from the USA for a big get-together to celebrate the heritage of jazz culture with a fresh perspective.

Teachers:

Workshops: Judith Novela for Afro Swing Kelly Isaak for Soft Shoe Tap More teachers for the Tasters TBA!
