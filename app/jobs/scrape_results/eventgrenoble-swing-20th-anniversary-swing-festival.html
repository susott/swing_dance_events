Grenoble Swing 20th Anniversary Swing Festival

  • When? 13th Sep 2024 - 15th Sep 2024
  • Country: France
  • Town: Grenoble
  • Website: https://www.grenobleswing.com/page/2763342-les-20-ans-the-20th-anniversary
  • Styles: Balboa, Blues, Charleston, Jazz, Lindy Hop, Shag

For it's 20th Anniversary, Grenoble Swing organises its biggest Jam! Four amazing bands will plays days and nights! 5 crazy parties, including 2 battle of the bands, 2 outdoor parties, and Jam session with the band, and of course, birthday celebrations! Come with us to celebrate the Swing, and Grenoble Swing 20th Anniversary!

Teachers:

Naomie and her Handsome Devils (USA) Gentlemen and Gangsters (Sweden) Bumpy Roof Band (Spain/France) Ubangi Men & the New Orleans Syncopators (France)
