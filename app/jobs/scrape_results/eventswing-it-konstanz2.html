Watervibes, birdsongs and happy feet! come and dance with us at the lake! Our sweet swing dance workshops include classes with different levels, parties, live music and lots of fun. You will benefit from experienced teachers that will spice your dance. The event takes place in spring and autumn. Friday Party with live concert (t.b.a.), Saturday live concert with Coniglio Connection, Sunday tea-dance. Save the Date! Registration opens approx. a month before the event.

Teachers:

Ben Taffner & Karin Gadziala (Germany) Pavli Grombiri Korcova (Czech Republic) & Victor Hristoskov (Bulgaria)
