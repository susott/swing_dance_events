Hülly Hop Swing Farm

  • When? 29th Nov 2024 - 1st Dec 2024
  • Country: Germany
  • Town: Drochtersen-Hüll near Hamburg
  • Website: https://swing.farm/
  • Styles: Lindy Hop

The Hülly Hop Swing Farm is located in the middle of nowhere (close enough to Hamburg). A fine cosy Lindy Hop Camp with international instructors, live music, accommodation and food. Up to 10 hours of learning time (4 hours of classes and up to 6 hours of tasters of your choice) 3 Parties with live music and DJ and free use of sauna

Teachers:

Genia & Arnas (Ukraine/Lithuania) and Güneş & Marcus (Germany)
